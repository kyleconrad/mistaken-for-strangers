Jai Paul - Jasmine

June 15, 2013 • Jadd Adams

Almost nothing was known about Jai Paul when his one and only single, “BTSTU” was released via his Myspace late in 2010, and not much has changed in two years. The mysterious Londoner took a unique approach to capitalizing on the track’s smashing success – he didn’t release anything and has barely been heard from since. That all changed two weeks ago with the release of “Jasmine,” a choppy, dark, sexy, R&B tinged morsel of dream pop. Jai Paul recently signed with XL Recordings, so maybe “Jasmine” is a sign of things to come. If so, it will be completely worth the two year wait.
